PRISM BIDS2DataLad Guide

This utility facilitates the ingestion of BIDS formatted MRI data into DataLad version-controlled repositories.

Workflow:
  1. Configure the Source (where your data is) and Destination (where you want the repository).
  2. Toggle Remote SSH if you are pushing to a central laboratory server.
  3. Click Start Conversion and monitor the console for real-time progress.
